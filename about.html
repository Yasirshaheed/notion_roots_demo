Who We Are

The Studio

A Kochi-based architecture and interiors practice built on craft, context, and purposeful design.

Our Story

Design + Build,
from Kochi.

Notion Roots was founded by Ar. Rafi Shakkeer with a simple conviction: that architecture should be rooted — in place, in purpose, and in the lives of the people it serves.

Operating out of Kochi, Kerala, we are a full-service design and build studio. We take projects from initial concept through architectural design, interior curation, civil construction, and specialised metalwork — all under one roof.

Every project, regardless of scale, receives the same rigorous attention to detail and the same commitment to delivering spaces that endure.
